Privacy Agreement

Please review and accept our FERPA-compliant privacy policy.

1. Overview

Tributary is committed to protecting the privacy of all users. This platform is designed for literacy leaders in K-12 education and does not collect, store, or process any student-level data.


2. Information We Collect

We collect only: name, email, professional role, district affiliation, and self-selected problem areas. District demographic data is sourced from publicly available NCES Common Core of Data.


3. FERPA Compliance

No student personally identifiable information (PII) is collected. All data pertains to district-level demographics and professional networking.


4. Data Usage

Your information is used to match you with peers facing similar challenges, facilitate communication, and improve platform features.


5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support.
